About KDVA Humphreys Chapter

🎯 Our Mission

To fortify the ROK-U.S. alliance through engaging activities including friendship events, educational outreach programs, and active community engagement within the Camp Humphreys community.

Who Can Join?

Membership is open to all who support the ROK-U.S. Alliance:

  • ✅ U.S. Service Members
  • ✅ DoD Civilians
  • ✅ Family Members
  • ✅ KATUSA Veterans
  • ✅ Alliance Supporters
